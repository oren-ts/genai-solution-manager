## 1 Initial situation

You are a **Junior** Prompt Engineer in a mid-sized tech company that develops AI-powered solutions for various industries. Your main task is to design and optimize effective prompts that support the following key business processes:

1. **Customer support**: Development of prompts for an AI-supported chatbot.
2. **Content creation**: Prompts for the generation of marketing texts and product descriptions.
3. **Data analysis**: Prompts to support data analysts in the interpretation of complex data sets.
4. **Software development**: Prompts to support developers with code documentation and error descriptions.  

---

## 2 Your task packages

### Task package 1: Prompt conception

1. Create a concept diagram that shows the different types of prompts for each business area and how they relate to each other.  
2. Develop a taxonomy for your prompts. Define categories and subcategories.  
3. Design a simple system for documenting your prompts that enables effective management.  

### Task package 2: Prompt development

1. Develop two basic prompts for each of the four business areas.  
2. Formulate a variation for each basic prompt to optimize performance in a specific context.  
3. Create a prompt quality checklist, e.g. to ensure that no unethical or biased wording is used.  

### Task package 3: Prompt analysis for decisions

1. Develop a concept for determining the two most effective prompts per business area. Define which metrics you would use for the evaluation.  
2. Design a system to identify the three most frequently used prompts based on frequency of use and average benefit to the user.  
3. Design a method to calculate the total benefit of prompts per quarter for the last year and to identify growth trends.  

### Task package 4: Prompt integrity and consistency checks

1. Develop a concept for determining the two most effective prompts per business area. Define which metrics you would use for the evaluation.  
2. Design a system to identify the three most frequently used prompts based on frequency of use and average benefit to the user.  
3. Design a method to calculate the total benefit of prompts per quarter for the last year and to identify growth trends.  

### Task package 5: Prompt interactions and user experience

1. Develop a concept for a flexible system that allows you to customize prompts based on user behavior and context.  
2. Design a method for collecting and analyzing user feedback on specific prompts.  
3. Outline a strategy for improving the user experience through optimized prompt sequences in complex interactions.  

---

## 3 Requirements

- Ensure that all prompt concepts are ethical, unbiased and inclusive.  
- Use best practices for structuring and documenting prompts to ensure clarity and traceability.  
- Pay attention to the potential efficiency of the prompts in your concepts, especially when processing complex requests.  
- Consider aspects of prompt integrity and security in your designs.  
- In your concepts, make sure to avoid inconsistencies in the prompt results and ensure the reliability of the system.
