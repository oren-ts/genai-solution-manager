### Preferred Model for Multimodal Research

**Choice:** **Google Gemini**

**Explanation:**
The textbook identifies Google Gemini as the model with "state-of-the-art multimodal capabilities," enabling it to process and link text, images, audio, and video content simultaneously (Chapter 3.2). This makes it uniquely qualified for research tasks involving different media types, as it can analyze complex relationships between these formats rather than processing text alone (Chapter 3.6). Additionally, its ability to access knowledge from trusted sources and handle large amounts of information supports the depth required for such research (Chapter 3.2, 3.5).
